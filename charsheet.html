Mortar and Pestle: During a short rest you can break down a very rare component into 2 rare components or break down a rare component into 2 common components.
Distill: During a short rest you can create a vial of solvent from an alcoholic beverage. Solvent can be used as a substitute for a common or rare component in a recipe.
       Acid
Acid (2C): As an action, you can splash the vial within 5 feet of you or throw the vial up to 20 feet. Splashing the vial on an object requires no check, but if you are throwing it or targeting a creature, then attempt an attack. On a hit, the target takes 2d6 acid damage.
       Alchemist's Fire
Alchemist's Fire (2R): This sticky fluid ignites when exposed to air. You can use an action to throw this mixture at a point within 30 feet. Each creature within 5 feet of the impact point must succeed on a DC 12 DEX save or take 2d6 fire damage at the start of each of its turns. A creature can end this damage by using its action to extinguish the flames.
       Invisibility Potion
Invisibility Potion (2VR): This potion's container looks empty but feels as though it holds liquid. When you drink it, you become invisible for 1 part of the day. Anything you wear or carry is invisible with you.
       Polymorph Potion
Polymorph Potion (5VR): When consumed, this potion randomly transforms the creature and their demeanor for 1 part of the day. If the creature dies or consumes the potion again while in this new form, they do not revert to their original form.
