Kill the Slog

Deoch replaces the turn-order grind with Unit Initiative. Combat is fast, tactical, and stays out of the way of the story.

Details
No more 20-minute waits for your turn. Unit Initiative allows the party and the GM to act in coordinated blocks, enabling synchronous tactical strikes and a fluid, fast-paced battlefield.

Proxy Roleplaying

Our Proxy Roll system lets mechanics inspire roleplay, making immersion accessible to every player at your table.

Details
Bridging the gap between player and character. Your rolls provide the spark for dialogue and action, turning mechanics into narrative momentum.

100% Core Stat-Based

Zero skill-bloat. No math-heavy proficiency bonuses. Every roll is intuitive, derived directly from your hero's core essence.

Details
Pure mechanical clarity. By removing the separate layer of "Skills," every action is tied directly to your core stats (Brawn, Agility, etc.), making the math instant and intuitive.

The Classes

Choose your path from ten distinct disciplines. Step into the dark and forge your legacy.

Barbarian

Embodiment of Instinct

Unleash primal fury and raw physical might. The Barbarian thrives in the heart of combat, shrugging off blows that would fell lesser warriors while dealing devastating strikes.

Druid

One with Nature

Guardians of the natural world. Druids harness the power of flora and fauna, shifting their forms and calling upon the elements to protect the balance of Tirnoch.

Fighter

Combat Tactician

Masters of weapon and shield. Fighters are the backbone of any party, combining martial discipline with tactical expertise to dominate the field of battle.

Monk

Master of Discipline

Disciplined warriors who weaponize their own life force. Monks strike with lightning speed and supernatural precision, maneuvering through combat with unmatched grace.

Paladin

Agent of Faith

Champions of divine will. Paladins are bulwarks of faith, shielding their allies and smiting their foes with celestial power fueled by their unwavering conviction.

Ranger

Commander of Nature

Expert survivalists and deadly marksmen. Rangers stalk their prey through the harshest environments, striking from the shadows before vanishing without a trace.

Rogue

Civilization Expert

Cunning scoundrels who excel at the unexpected. Rogues disable traps, pick locks, and exploit enemy weaknesses to deliver lethal sneak attacks.

Sorcerer

Avatar of The Weave

Vessels of raw, untamed magic. Sorcerers draw upon their powerful bloodlines to bend reality, unleashing explosive spells born of sheer willpower.

Wizard

Academic of Arcana

Students of the arcane arts. Wizards rely on years of study and their spellbooks to cast a vast array of magics, offering unmatched utility and control.

Psychic

Master of Mind

Masters of the mind. Psychics weaponize thought itself, shattering enemy resolve, predicting attacks, and manipulating the battlefield through sheer cognition.
