Design Philosophy

Kill the Slog

Initiative & Combat Flow

Deoch replaces the turn-order grind with Unit Initiative. Combat is fast, tactical, and stays out of the way of the story. No more 20-minute waits for your turn. Unit Initiative allows the party and the GM to act in coordinated blocks, enabling synchronous tactical strikes and a fluid, fast-paced battlefield.

Proxy Roleplaying

Immersive Narratives

Our Proxy Roll system lets mechanics inspire roleplay, making immersion accessible to every player at your table. Bridging the gap between player and character. Your rolls provide the spark for dialogue and action, turning mechanics into narrative momentum.

100% Core Stat-Based

Intuitive Actions

Zero skill-bloat. No math-heavy proficiency bonuses. Every roll is intuitive, derived from your hero's core stats. By removing the separate layer of "Skills," every action is tied directly to your core stats making the math instant and intuitive.

The Arcane Toll

Elfrot Corruption

Magic is a rare and volatile force. Wielding it alone is a risk. Every spell cast taints the soul leading to eventual death.. Do you tempt fate?

Bloodlines & Mastery

Progression & Legacy

Your origin defines your traits, but your will defines your legacy. No two heroes are ever truly alike. Mastery awaits those willing to acheive it.
